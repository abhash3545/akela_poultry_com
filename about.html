About Us

Quality-focused poultry farming in Giridih, Jharkhand

Akela Poultry Farm is a poultry business based in Giridih, Jharkhand, engaged in poultry farming and supply of quality poultry products. We focus on healthy bird management, hygienic farming practices, and reliable supply to farmers, traders, and customers.

Our aim is to provide consistent quality, fair pricing, and dependable service in the poultry sector across Giridih, Deoghar, Barhi, Chatra, and Jamua.
